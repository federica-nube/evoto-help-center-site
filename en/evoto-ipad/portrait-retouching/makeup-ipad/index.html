iPad Portrait Retouching – Makeup

Evoto's Makeup module automatically detects facial features and intelligently applies makeup to appropriate areas of the face. This module supports enhancing existing makeup or adding new makeup when none is present. You can fine-tune each area individually or together, depending on your needs. General Enhancements Highlights Enhances the natural highlights on the face to make it appear more luminous and three-dimensional. Use the slider to increase or decrease the effect. Contour Adds depth to the face by enhancing shadows. This helps define and emphasize facial structure. Adjust the slider to control the intensity of the contouring. Advanced Adjustments These features provide detailed control over existing makeup on the eyebrows, eyes, and lips. All adjustments support both conjunction (linked) and separate (individual left/right) editing.

Eyebrow Makeup Enhances or reduces the existing eyebrow makeup. Slide the left to reduce the effect, or slide right to enhance it. Separate adjustments for the left and right eyebrows are supported. Eye Makeup Enhances the original eye makeup: Amount: Enhances or reduces the existing Eye makeup. Slide to the left to reduce the amount, or slide to the right to enhance it. The following sliders become active once theAmountis adjusted Saturation: Adjusts the intensity of the eye makeup color. Slide left to mute the color, or right to brighten it. Luminance: Adjusts the lightness of the eye makeup. Dimensionality: Enhances the 3D effect, making the eyes appear more prominent. Lip Makeup Refines the lipstick effect with additional control. Amount: Enhances or reduces the existing Lip makeup. Slide to the left to reduce the amount, or slide to the right to enhance it. The following sliders become active once theAmountis adjusted Saturation: Adjusts the intensity of the lip color. Slide left to reduce the color; slide right to intensify it. Luminance: Controls the lightness of the lipstick. Dimensionality: Increases the lip’s three-dimensional appearance for a fuller look. Makeup Looks Evoto offers a wide selection of pre-designed makeup styles that can either replace or enhance the character's existing makeup. Each style includes an amount slider to control how much of the look is applied. Makeup Suite Includes 18 professionally designed makeup looks, including 2 makeup styles designed specifically for male portraits, one for tweens and 2 for younger children.

Individual Makeup Components EyebrowsThere are currently 9 different eyebrow makeup styles available. Users can adjust both eyebrows together or individually. Position adjustments are not supported within the Makeup module. However, the eyebrow and eyelash materials can be split for independent left and right adjustments. EyeshadowThere are 8 eyeshadow styles to choose from, designed to suit a range of lighting and skin tones. EyelashesEvoto provides 14 different eyelash makeup styles. Like the eyebrows, these can be adjusted together or independently for the left and right eyes. EyelinerThere are 6 eyeliner designs available, offering various styles and intensities to complement different eye shapes. ContactsYou can choose from 11 different contact lens effects to change the color or style of the character’s eyes. BlushThere are 12 blush styles to choose from. Styles No. 10 to 12 have updated icons and are marked as suitable for children or infants. LipstickChoose from 15 different lip colors. After selecting a color, you can also adjust the texture of the lipstick to better match the desired look and finish.

ContourThere are 6 contour makeup looks available, designed to shape and define the face subtly or dramatically. Face DecorationsEvoto currently offers 6 face decoration options, all of which are freckle styles.
