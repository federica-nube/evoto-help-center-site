Evoto provides powerful AI-driven tools to enhance your pet photos. The Pet Body Retouching features help you achieve professional results by automatically removing distractions and refining your pet's appearance.

Leash Removal#

The Pet Leash Removal feature allows for the automatic detection and removal of leashes in pet photography while preserving the texture and background detail of the original image.

Click the Apply button and the AI will automatically detect and remove leashes from your pet photo.

This tool generates a new .tsq format file and charges 1 credit when you export the generated new image version. The new image retains all previously applied edits. To find generated images, use the Library Filter and select "Others".

Notes: Supports single image processing only (no batch processing). Optimized for leashes and traction ropes. For collars or harnesses, use the Healing Brush manually.

Stray Fur Removal#

Automatically clean up pet photos by eliminating distracting flyaways and frizz. This feature quickly detects and minimizes high-frequency stray hairs while preserving the natural texture of the pet's coat.

Adjust the slider to control intensity. 0 applies no effect, while 100 provides maximum removal of stray hairs.

Note: You can use the manual brush to adjust the application area of the effect for more precise control.
