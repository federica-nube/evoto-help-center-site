Portrait Retouching – Full Body Reshape

Evoto’s Full Body Reshape module uses advanced AI and 3D skeleton point positioning to enhance body proportions while preserving natural appearance. Adjustments are intelligently applied based on body posture and composition, offering subtle to dramatic reshaping options. AI Reshape Evoto uses 3D skeleton point positioning combined with posture recognition to generate a natural slimming or plumping effect. This feature preserves the original body shape and maintains a realistic appearance. Note: AI Reshape may be automatically disabled for group photos to avoid interference from overlapping figures. Smooth Physique This feature smooths unnatural protrusions on body areas (excluding clothing), refining the overall body silhouette. Only works when AI Reshape is active with a negative value (from -1 to -100). To apply smoothing without slimming, set AI Reshape to -1.

Individual Body Adjustments Body – Adjusts overall body shape for a slimmer or fuller appearance. Also subtly affects head-to-body ratio. Height – Increases overall body height. Note: May be disabled in non-full-body images. Upper Body Length – adjust the length of the upper body—from the shoulders to the top of the torso—to help refine the overall proportions. Head – Adjusts head size (including hair). Note: May be disabled in group image Neck Width – Adjusts neck dimensions. Supports both unified and individual side adjustments. Neck Length – Range: -100 to +100 (default: 0). Slide left to shorten, right to lengthen. Arms – Adjust arm thickness. Note: May be disabled in group images Breasts – Resize the chest area using the slider. WaistWidth– Slim the waistline. May be disabled in group images. Supports unified or individual side adjustments. Waist Length –focuses on the length of the waist area and can be used to subtly adjust a subject’s total height and torso proportions. Hips – Adjust hip width. Supports unified or individual side adjustments. Note: May be disabled in group images Leg Width – Slims leg shape. May be unavailable in non-full-body photos. (Note: By default (without clicking the link icon), thighs and calves can be adjusted separately. When the link icon is selected, an unified "Amount" slider replaces individual controls, synchronizing adjustments for both thighs and calves). Leg Length – Lengthens the legs, enhancing thigh-to-calf ratio based on the golden ratio (3:5). When on, it will activate sub-sliders to control thigh and calf adjustments separately. (Note: Adjusting the main 'Leg Length' slider activates the sub-sliders for manual thigh/calf refinement. By default, the lengthening ratio between thighs and calves is set to 80:100).
