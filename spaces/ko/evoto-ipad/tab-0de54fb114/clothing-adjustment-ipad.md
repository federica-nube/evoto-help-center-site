**De-wrinkle Clothing** The **De-wrinkle Clothing** feature in Evoto allows you to effectively remove wrinkles from fabric while meticulously preserving its original texture and natural three-dimensional structure. This tool offers flexible adjustment to minimize fine creases without flattening the clothing's realistic appearance. ***Note:****Gender and age options are available to support more individualized de-wrinkling adjustments. These options allow for tailored results based on the subject’s characteristics, ensuring a more natural outcome.* ![image.png](https://res.evoto.ai/community/post/5i2UXneT2Cd.png) **Sub-features** These additional refinement options become available only after the main **De-wrinkle Clothing** feature is enabled. **Remove Fine Wrinkles:** This tool is designed to eliminate subtle, thin creases and minor imperfections in clothing. Use the slider to precisely control the intensity of this effect, allowing for gentle smoothing or more pronounced wrinkle removal. **Remove Coarse Wrinkles:** This feature focuses on reducing larger creases, prominent folds, and uneven lighting or shadow areas that detract from the fabric's appearance. Use the slider to adjust the amount of reduction applied, effectively smoothing out significant imperfections for a polished look. **De-Blemish Clothing** This tool removes lint, dust, flakes, and other small distractions from clothing using a single slider to control intensity. **Clothing Edge Smoothing** ![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Screenshot-2025-06-26-at-16.04.45-300x300.png) Smooths out wrinkles and creases at the edges of clothing using AI. Automatically activates: *Clothing Edge Refinement*: Fine-tune specific edge areas with the manual tuning pen ![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Screenshot-2025-06-26-at-16.05.49-300x253.png) *Objects Protection*: Prevents distortion of nearby objects ![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Screenshot-2025-06-26-at-16.06.50-300x118.png) **Manual Tuning Pen for Clothing Touch-Up** Use the Manual Tuning Pen to brush or erase the effects of Clothing & Accessories adjustments. This allows you to restore any areas that were mistakenly affected after applying the touch-up. ![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Screenshot-2025-06-26-at-16.07.56-300x233.png)
