Want a brighter smile in your portraits? Evoto makes teeth whitening simple with a few different tools. Here are **three easy methods** you can use:

#### 1. Teeth Whitening Slider

The fastest method is built right into **Portrait Retouch > Teeth**.

- Open your image in Evoto.
- Go to **Portrait Retouch > Teeth**.
- Adjust the **Brightness** slider to lighten the teeth.
- Use **Teeth Desaturation** to remove yellow tones. ???? This is the most precise way to instantly enhance a smile.

![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/teeth-natural-scaled.jpg)

#### 2. Smile Generation with Whitening Effect

Sometimes, just adding a brighter smile helps the teeth appear whiter:

- Navigate to **Portrait Retouching > Facial Expression**.
- Select **Smile Generation** and choose the **Toothy** option.
- This naturally shows more teeth, and when combined with whitening, makes portraits pop. ???? Ideal for giving subjects both a confident smile and enhanced brightness.

#### 3. Color Adjustment + Teeth Masking

For more control, you can use masking:

- Head to **Color Adjustment > Masking**.
- From the drop-down, select **Teeth** – Evoto will automatically detect and mask them.

![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/mask-scaled.jpg)

- Adjust **Brightness** and fine-tune the **Color sliders** for a natural look. ???? Great if you want to control the overall tone and whiteness more manually.

✨ With these three methods, you can quickly bring out radiant, natural-looking smiles in any portrait. Whether you want one-click whitening or total control, Evoto has you covered.
