Adjust subtle facial expressions to enhance mood and natural appearance in portraits. These tools are ideal for creating more inviting or polished facial expressions while maintaining realism.

##### Gummy Smile

Control the amount of gum exposure by adjusting the upper lip position. This is useful for reducing the appearance of a "gummy smile" or minimizing protruding teeth.
- The left and right sides of the upper lip can be adjusted independently for more precise correction.

Gentle Smile Enhance the corners of the mouth to introduce a subtle smile. This effect focuses only on mouth shape and does not influence other facial features. Smile Generation Toothy Generate a natural-looking smile that adapts to the face in real time. Offers five levels of intensity, ranging from a slight smile to a full laugh. Focuses on the mouth area and adjusts the corners to create expressive variations. Neutral (Smile Reversal) For scenarios like identification photos (such as baby ID photos) and high-end portraits, the **Neutral** option provides the ability to adjust smiles to neutral expressions or closed-mouth states. This helps users with protruding mouths or unattractive teeth naturally close their mouths in photos. Three intensity levels are provided (0-3), with the default set to level 3. **Level 1:** Open mouth situation - intermediate state. **Level 2:** Closed mouth, mouth corner slightly lower - more constrained expression. **Level 3:** Closed mouth, eyes open, apple cheek more pronounced - most serious expression. Select "None" to clear all smile generation effects. *Note: This feature will auto-disable when pouting, overly open mouth, strange expression, large side profile, or severely obstructed mouth is detected. Manual Tuning Pen is supported on iPad to fix areas affected incorrectly.*
