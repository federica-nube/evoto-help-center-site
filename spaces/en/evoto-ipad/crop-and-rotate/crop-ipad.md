The Crop module in Evoto for iPad provides flexible cropping options to suit various editing needs, including fixed ratios, custom dimensions, and resolution-based inputs.

---

### Crop Modes

Evoto supports three cropping methods:

- **Fixed Ratio**Choose from standard aspect ratios to maintain proportional dimensions.
- **Free Sizing**Manually adjust the crop area without any ratio constraints.
- **Input Size (W × H × Resolution)**Enter specific width, height, and resolution values for precise cropping.
- **Unit:** Inches
- Ideal for print or specific output requirements.

Once cropping is complete, tap **OK** in the bottom right corner to apply and save the crop.

### Seamless Tab Switching

While cropping, users can switch between different editing tabs without interrupting the crop process. This allows for smoother and more flexible workflow adjustments.

---

### Aspect Ratio Options

- **Original**Maintains the dimensions of the image as imported into Evoto.
- **As Shot**Crops based on the image’s original capture size.
- **W × H × Resolution**Allows manual input of exact dimensions and resolution.

Additional options:

- **Aspect Ratio Button**Tap to switch between aspect ratios quickly.
- **Link Button**Tap to lock the aspect ratio while resizing the crop box.

---

**Standard Cropping Sizes**

Evoto provides a list of common standard sizes for quick selection. These are ideal for prints, social media, or other platform-specific dimensions.
