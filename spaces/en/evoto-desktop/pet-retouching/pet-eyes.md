Evoto offers AI-powered tools to enhance your pet's eyes, bringing out their natural beauty and expressiveness with just a few adjustments.

---

### Eye Lightening

Brighten and enhance the natural luminosity of your pet's eyes. This feature subtly increases eye brightness to make them appear more vibrant and engaging.

![Evoto Pet Retouching - Eyes Panel](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Snipaste_2025-12-09_17-03-19.jpg)

Adjust the slider to control the brightness level of your pet's eyes.

> Note: You can unlock the link icon to adjust the left and right eyes separately for more precise control.

### Eye Color Enhancement

Intensify and enrich the natural color of your pet's eyes. This tool enhances the existing eye color to make it more vivid and captivating.

Adjust the slider to control the color intensity.

> Note: You can unlock the link icon to adjust the left and right eyes separately for more precise control.

---

### Catchlights

Add realistic light reflections to your pet's eyes. Catchlights create that spark of life that makes eyes appear more lively and engaging.

![Evoto Pet Retouching - Catchlights Settings](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Snipaste_2025-12-09_17-23-22.jpg)

Choose a category (**General**, **Indoors**, or **Outdoors**) and select a catchlight style from the available presets.

> Note: Supports batch processing and sync adjustments across multiple images.

- **Amount -** controls the opacity and brightness of the catchlight reflection.
- **Horizontal -** shifts the catchlight left or right across the eye.
- **Vertical -** shifts the catchlight up or down within the eye.
- **Rotate -** changes the rotation or angle of the catchlight shape.
- **Flip Horizontal -** mirrors the catchlight effect horizontally for the selected eye.

> Note: You can unlock the link icon to adjust the left and right eyes separately for more precise control.
