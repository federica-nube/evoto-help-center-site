De-wrinkle Clothing Use the slider to remove wrinkles from clothing and restore the original fabric texture. The effect supports flexible adjustment to preserve the natural three-dimensional structure of the clothing while minimizing fine creases. ***Note:*** Gender and age options are available to support more individualized de-wrinkling adjustments based on the subject’s characteristics.

![image.png](https://res.evoto.ai/community/post/5i2UXneT2Cd.png)

Sub-features These options are only available after De-wrinkle Clothing is enabled. **Remove Fine Wrinkles**Eliminate thin creases in clothing. Use the slider to control the intensity of the effect. **Remove Coarse Wrinkles**Reduce large creases and uneven lighting or shadow areas on fabric. Use the slider to adjust the amount applied. De-Blemish Clothing

![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Screenshot-2025-06-26-at-16.03.05-300x300.png)

This tool removes lint, dust, flakes, and other small distractions from clothing using a single slider to control intensity. Clothing Edge Smoothing

![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Screenshot-2025-06-26-at-16.04.45-300x300.png)

Smooths out wrinkles and creases at the edges of clothing using AI. Automatically activates: *Clothing Edge Refinement*: Fine-tune specific edge areas with the manual tuning pen

![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Screenshot-2025-06-26-at-16.05.49-300x253.png)

*Objects Protection*: Prevents distortion of nearby objects

![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Screenshot-2025-06-26-at-16.06.50-300x118.png)

Manual Tuning Pen for Clothing Touch-Up Use the Manual Tuning Pen to brush or erase the effects of Clothing & Accessories adjustments. This allows you to restore any areas that were mistakenly affected after applying the touch-up.

![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Screenshot-2025-06-26-at-16.07.56-300x233.png)
