Evoto is committed to serving photographers around the world with intelligent, high-quality editing solutions. To better support our global user base, we’ve expanded our reach with international branches and continue to develop localized versions of our software.
Evoto currently supports
12 different languages
, and our team is actively working to expand this list as we grow into new regions.
**Change Language** To change the language of the interface: Click on **Evoto** in the top menu. Select **Language**. Choose your preferred language from the list. ![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/截屏2025-05-27-14.06.39-300x284.png) The interface will update automatically to reflect your selection.
