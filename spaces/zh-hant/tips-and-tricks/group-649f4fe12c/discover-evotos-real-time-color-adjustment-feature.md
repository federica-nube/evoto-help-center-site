Evoto is designed to give you precise control over your photo edits, and one of its standout features is the **Real-Time Color Adjustment** tool. This powerful functionality allows you to focus specifically on color changes while temporarily hiding other non-color-related effects, streamlining your editing process.

### What is Real-Time Color Adjustment?

The **Real-Time Color Adjustment** feature isolates your color modifications from other applied editing effects. This means you can instantly adjust and view color changes without being influenced by other effects, such as retouching, liquify, or sharpening. It's especially helpful when fine-tuning color grading or assessing the impact of specific color edits.

![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Screenshot-2025-08-14-at-11.43.09-300x174.png)

### How It Works

- **Enabling Real-Time Color Adjustment:** When this feature is turned on, Evoto will hide all non-color-related effects. This allows you to focus solely on how your color adjustments are impacting the image. It’s an ideal mode for fine-tuning hues, saturation, and other color settings without any distractions.
- **Disabling Real-Time Color Adjustment:** If you prefer to view all effects applied to your image, simply turn off this feature. While this mode provides a comprehensive view of how all effects interact, keep in mind that it might take slightly longer for color adjustments to be reflected due to the additional processing of all active effects.

### Why Use Real-Time Color Adjustment?

This feature offers several key advantages for your editing workflow:

- **Focused Editing:** By isolating color adjustments, you can make more precise edits and see the impact of your changes immediately, without the interference of other effects.
- **Efficiency:** This feature speeds up the process of color correction and enhancement, making your workflow more efficient, especially when working with complex images.
- **Better Results:** When color adjustments are made in real-time, you achieve a more accurate and satisfying result, as you can see how changes affect the image clearly and quickly.

The **Real-Time Color Adjustment** feature is a valuable tool for any photo editor who wants to fine-tune colors with precision. Turn it on to focus on color, or turn it off to view all effects—whichever best suits your editing needs.
