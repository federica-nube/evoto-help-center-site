電腦設定 對於mac 15.0以上系統，需要在隱私權權限>中開啟檔案及資料夾權限可刪除檔案權限

![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/image-300x261.jpg)

![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/image1-300x261.jpg)

相機設定 關閉**省電模式**（開啟省電模式後，一段時間內沒有操作相機，相機將進入睡眠模式並斷開連接）。 以佳能 EOS R6 為例，開啟**省電設置**，將**顯示關閉**時間設定為**最大**，**自動關機**選擇為**停用**。

![](https://evoto.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3099291537-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FGcGHhe6Z57mAjGmYtrQ4%252Fuploads%252FSx4N7uyP8ZfsRTKqsXxA%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D49bf0265-da91-4105-9b96-74da054ad646&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=6f98a52a&sv=2)

**生態模式**設定為**關閉**。

![](https://evoto.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3099291537-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FGcGHhe6Z57mAjGmYtrQ4%252Fuploads%252FZ9ZzcRyRI0LFTMvDoCiH%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D919f42c2-4ed5-4828-8218-a6a9830d0f09&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=bb5f4c32&sv=2)

某些相機，例如 R6 II、R8 和 R5 M2，需要設定 **USB 連接應用程式** >**照片匯入/遠端控制**。

![](https://evoto.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3099291537-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FGcGHhe6Z57mAjGmYtrQ4%252Fuploads%252Fz9u0Qu4yCf9RxiSrVBjX%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Ddaf5c0fb-9e5c-4f40-8aae-6de7c0414e07&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=c5dac9fb&sv=2)

部分相機如Canon5D4，需要連接電腦USB 3.0以上標準介面才能夠正常連線。連接至USB 2.0介面時，會出現連線速度慢、無法開啟即時預覽、影像傳輸速度慢等問題。 尋找 usb3.0 圖標，

![](https://evoto.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3099291537-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FGcGHhe6Z57mAjGmYtrQ4%252Fuploads%252FSr2iKqOv12eK7Lk7yspq%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3De5a79cc1-4517-4349-902b-2cf1427e0a4b&width=77&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=a944dd6f&sv=2)

或檢查 usb 介面的顏色，它是藍色的

![](https://evoto.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3099291537-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FGcGHhe6Z57mAjGmYtrQ4%252Fuploads%252F2SXhDs1CmznC2B1mMU6L%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D7d812c18-1a22-4ef2-b2c3-e79d67c66fd1&width=83&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=3e712991&sv=2)

電腦上的USB-Type C介面可用於網路線上拍攝。
