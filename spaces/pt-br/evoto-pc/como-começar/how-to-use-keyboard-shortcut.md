To quickly access a full list of keyboard shortcuts available in Evoto: Click the **вқ“ (Question Mark)** icon located in the bottom right corner of the interface to open the **Customer Support Center** menu. Select **Keyboard Shortcuts** to open a pop-up window displaying all available shortcuts. ![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/жҲӘеұҸ2025-05-27-10.56.03-236x300.png) These shortcuts are designed to streamline your workflow and help you edit more efficiently. ![](https://support.evoto.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/жҲӘеұҸ2025-05-27-14.55.06-300x185.png)
