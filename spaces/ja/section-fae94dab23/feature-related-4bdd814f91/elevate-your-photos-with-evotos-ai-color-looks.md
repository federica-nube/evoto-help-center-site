Evoto’s innovative **AI Color Looks** feature is designed to elevate your photos with personalized color enhancements, all with just a click. This advanced AI tool intelligently adapts to the specific elements of each photo, ensuring that the selected "Look" aligns seamlessly with the original essence of your image.

#### Achieving Desired Effects with a Click

With the **AI Color Looks** feature, achieving the perfect photo effect has never been easier. A single click is all it takes to apply your preferred Look, as the AI-driven algorithm automatically fine-tunes the photo’s color settings. You can then use the **Amount** slider to precisely control the intensity of the effect.

- **Dynamic Adjustments:** The AI will produce varying adjustment parameters depending on the specific qualities of each image, ensuring the color adjustments are perfectly suited to that photo.
- **Refresh the Look:** If you want to see an alternative interpretation of a Look, simply click the **Refresh** button. This will generate a new set of color adjustments for the same selected Look.

#### Intelligent Adjustments Tailored to Your Photo

The **AI Color Looks** feature goes beyond basic color adjustments. The AI strives to achieve an optimal color balance that complements the selected Look, all while preserving the original qualities of the photo.

- **Precise Exposure and White Balance Adjustments:** The AI precisely modifies the exposure and white balance, enhancing the realism of the image’s colors.
- **Intelligent Subject Enhancement:** The feature has been upgraded to better highlight the subject in the image, often eliminating the need for users to create additional local masks.

#### Efficient Batch Processing for Consistent Style

When working with a collection of photos that need a uniform Look, the **AI Color Looks** feature offers an efficient solution. It enables quick batch processing, allowing you to apply consistent color adjustments across multiple images with minimal effort. This not only saves time but also ensures that each photo retains its unique attributes while adopting the desired Look style.

The **AI Color Looks** feature in Evoto is a powerful tool for photographers seeking to enhance their images with minimal effort while maintaining the integrity of the original photo. Whether you’re working with a single image or a batch of photos, this feature ensures that your final results are consistent, natural, and beautifully aligned with your creative vision.
